Optimizer Experiments for Transformer-Style Models

Exploratory work on making Muon / Polar-Express–style optimizers pluggable inside a GPT-2–like training loop, with extra metrics for orthogonality, attention statistics, and optimizer-step diagnostics.

  • Goal: compare AdamW vs. Muon / Polar-Express–like updates on small/medium models
  • Logged activation norms, LR schedules, and per-layer updates to inspect stability
  • Used linear algebra tools to reason about update structure (matrix-sign / polar-style steps)
